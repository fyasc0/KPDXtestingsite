Portland's Guild Wars 2 Guild

We are a local, socially-oriented Portland, Oregon Guild Wars 2 guild focused on connecting gamers around the Portland area through meetups and group events. Our group is very supportive in and out of game, seeking to help guildmates achieve any goals they have for their Guild Wars 2 gaming experience.
Must be 18+ years and a legal adult.
